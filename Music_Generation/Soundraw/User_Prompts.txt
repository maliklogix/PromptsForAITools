# User Prompts for Soundraw

## Prompt 1: YouTube Tech Review Background
"Generate a modern, clean electronic track for a tech product review YouTube video. The mood should be 'innovative and confident' — think sleek product unboxing. Medium energy with a consistent groove that won't distract from narration. Instrumentation: subtle synth pads, clean electric guitar, light electronic drums, and a warm bass line. Tempo: 100-110 BPM. I need it in 3 minutes with this energy map: low energy intro (15s) → medium energy for the main review (2 min) → slightly higher energy for the verdict section (30s) → fade out (15s). The track should sit well under a male voiceover without frequency clashing."

## Prompt 2: Instagram Reels Fitness Content
"Create a high-energy workout music track for Instagram Reels. Genre: EDM/future bass with a powerful drop. The hook MUST hit within the first 2 seconds — no long intros. Heavy bass, punchy drums, energetic synth leads, and a build-up to a satisfying drop. Tempo: 130-140 BPM. Duration: 30 seconds. Energy level: maximum throughout. Think of the kind of music that makes someone want to stop scrolling and start working out. The beat should sync well with quick-cut workout clips. Include a rhythmic section where the beat drops out briefly for a vocal-sync moment."

## Prompt 3: Podcast Intro and Background Bundle
"I need a music package for my business podcast called 'The Growth Lab.' Create: (1) A 12-second intro theme — confident, professional, modern corporate feel with clean guitars, warm pads, and a subtle beat. Should start strong and end with a clean cut. (2) A 10-second outro — same melody but softer, winding down to a clean ending. (3) A 3-minute low-energy background loop for interview segments — barely-there ambient pads and very subtle rhythm, designed to sit far under speech without any distracting melodic elements. All three should share the same sonic palette and feel cohesive as a brand package. Key: D major, mood: professional, innovative, trustworthy."

## Prompt 4: Travel Vlog Adventure Music
"Generate an uplifting, adventurous track for a travel vlog showing a road trip through Iceland. The music should feel vast, wonder-filled, and slightly epic — like discovering something magical. Instrumentation: acoustic guitar, atmospheric synths, gentle piano, building percussion (tribal/world-style drums), and a soaring melodic hook with strings. Tempo: 95 BPM. Duration: 4 minutes. Energy map: quiet, atmospheric opening (30s, drone shots of landscapes) → medium energy with rhythm (1 min, driving scenes) → high energy with full instrumentation (1 min, adventure activities) → medium-high sustain (1 min, more travel) → gentle wind-down outro (30s, sunset shots). This should feel cinematic but still personal."

## Prompt 5: E-Commerce Product Ad
"Create a trendy, catchy track for a 30-second e-commerce product ad on TikTok and Instagram. The product is a premium skincare line targeting 25-35 year old women. Genre: modern pop/tropical house with a feel-good summer vibe. Tempo: 115 BPM. Bright, airy synths, finger snaps or claps, a playful marimba or kalimba melody, and a light, bouncy bass. The track should feel luxurious but approachable — aspirational without being intimidating. Energy curve: medium-high start → build to a satisfying drop at 15 seconds (product reveal) → sustain energy → clean ending with a 'button' ending suitable for logo placement."

## Prompt 6: Relaxation and Study Lo-Fi
"Generate a lo-fi chill track for a 'study with me' YouTube video. Classic lo-fi aesthetic: dusty vinyl crackle, jazzy piano chords (Fmaj7 - Dm7 - Bbmaj7 - C type progression), mellow boom-bap drums with a lazy swing, warm analog bass, and occasional jazz guitar licks. Tempo: 72 BPM. Duration: 5 minutes, seamlessly loopable. Energy: consistently low and mellow — no big changes, no surprising elements. The track should feel like a cozy coffee shop at midnight. Keep it minimal enough that it fades into the background but interesting enough to enjoy on its own. Add subtle ambient sounds: rain or distant city hum."
