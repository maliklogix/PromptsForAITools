# Soundraw — Prompt Guide

## Overview
Soundraw is an AI music generator built specifically for content creators. It creates original, royalty-free music that users can customize in real-time — adjusting energy levels, instruments, tempo, and structure per section. Soundraw is designed for YouTubers, podcasters, filmmakers, and social media creators who need music that fits their content perfectly.

## How to Use

### System Prompt
1. Use `System_Prompt.txt` to configure AI music generation workflows for content creation platforms.
2. Adapt it for building custom music recommendation or generation tools.

### User Prompts
- Use Soundraw at **soundraw.io** by selecting mood, genre, and energy level.
- Fine-tune generated tracks by toggling instruments and adjusting energy per section.
- Download tracks for use in videos, podcasts, and ads.

## Tips for Best Results
- **Describe your content type** — "YouTube tech review" gives different results than "Instagram Reel."
- **Specify energy levels** — tell the AI where you need high, medium, and low energy.
- **Mention the platform** — YouTube, TikTok, and podcast each need different music styles.
- **Request specific durations** — match the music length to your content length.
- **Describe what's under the music** — voiceover, dialogue, or standalone changes the mix needs.
- **Customize after generation** — use Soundraw's section editor to fine-tune each part.
