# Suno — Prompt Guide

## Overview
Suno is an AI-powered music generation platform that creates complete songs — including vocals, lyrics, instrumentation, and production — from simple text prompts. It can produce radio-quality tracks in virtually any genre within seconds, making music creation accessible to everyone regardless of musical background.

## How to Use

### System Prompt
1. Use `System_Prompt.txt` to configure AI music generation workflows or chatbot integrations.
2. Adapt it for building custom music creation interfaces or prompt optimization tools.

### User Prompts
- Enter prompts directly at **suno.com** or through the Suno API.
- Be specific about genre, mood, tempo, vocals, and instrumentation for best results.
- Use the "Custom" mode to provide your own lyrics alongside style descriptions.

## Tips for Best Results
- **Specify the genre clearly** — "indie folk" gives very different results from "folk rock."
- **Describe the mood** — emotional descriptors like "melancholic," "triumphant," or "dreamy" shape the entire production.
- **Mention reference artists** — saying "in the style of Billie Eilish" helps dial in the sound.
- **Include structure cues** — use brackets like `[Verse]`, `[Chorus]`, `[Bridge]` in custom lyrics.
- **Set tempo and key** — specifying "120 BPM in A minor" gives you precise control.
- **Iterate** — generate multiple versions and remix your favorites.
