# Udio — Prompt Guide

## Overview
Udio is an AI music generation platform known for producing exceptionally high-fidelity audio. It creates complete songs with realistic vocals, accurate genre reproduction, and professional-grade production quality. Udio supports generation, extension, and remixing of tracks across virtually every genre and era.

## How to Use

### System Prompt
1. Use `System_Prompt.txt` to configure AI music generation workflows or build music creation tools.
2. Adapt it for any AI-powered music production pipeline.

### User Prompts
- Enter prompts directly at **udio.com** or through the API.
- Specify genre, sub-genre, era, and production style for precise results.
- Use the extension feature to build songs section by section.

## Tips for Best Results
- **Be genre-specific** — "post-punk revival" gives better results than just "rock."
- **Reference specific eras** — "80s synth-pop production" tells the AI exactly what sonic palette to use.
- **Describe the vocal style** — breathy, raspy, belting, falsetto — be specific about delivery.
- **Use musical terminology** — mention BPM, key, chord progressions, and arrangement details.
- **Build iteratively** — generate the first section, then extend to build the full song.
- **Describe the recording environment** — "sounds like a garage recording" vs. "polished studio production."
